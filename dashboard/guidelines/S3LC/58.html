Consensus-based recommendation EEC Radiochemotherapy should only be administered at facilities where acute toxicities caused by radiotherapy or chemotherapy can be detected and adequately treated (facilities that meet the relevant requirements of the certification for head and neck cancer centers). Evidence-based recommendation Recommendation level In patients with locally advanced laryngeal carcinoma (M0) who are not treated with surgery, primary chemoradiotherapy should be preferred to radiotherapy alone, especially in the age group up to 70 years.
